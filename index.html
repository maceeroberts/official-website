Thesis Research

My thesis project is looking at resource partitioning, specifically in endangered gray bats (Myotis grisescens). I'm interested in how habitat characteristics and environmental conditions drive foraging activity. I am deploying acoustic detectors on Fort Campbell Military Installation. In my first field season this past summer, I deployed a total of 44 acoustics.
